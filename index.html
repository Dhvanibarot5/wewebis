About This Insurance
Product Policy

Multistreams’ Diaspora Cover is a renewable insurance offering that combines hospitalisation, and cash benefits in the events of illness, accident or death of the life insured.

You never know what life's going to throw your way, but by protecting yourself and loved ones with a diaspora cover, you can at least have some peace of mind knowing that, if something happens to you, you'll be covered. With life insurance, the stress of wondering how you're going to cover hospital bills, or how your family will pay for your funeral should the worst happen are taken away.
